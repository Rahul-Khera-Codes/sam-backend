The Character

A live AI avatar, not just a chat box

The Executive Agent has a visual identity — an animated character that lives on the screen while you talk. It reacts in real time: it shows when it's listening to you, when it's working something out, and when it's speaking back. This makes the interaction feel like talking to an actual assistant rather than typing into a form.

Listening
Microphone is active. The agent is waiting for your command.
Thinking
Processing your request, checking connected services.
Speaking
Responding out loud. Results or previews appear alongside.
Phase 1 ships with a clean animated avatar showing these three states. Phase 2 adds a more expressive face, customisable appearance, and personality settings the business owner can configure.
How It Works

Four steps, start to finish

01
Open a session
Click the Executive Agent page in the portal. Your microphone activates and the agent is ready — no setup, no phone number needed.
02
Speak or type a command
Describe what you need in plain English. "Draft a reply to the last email from John." "What appointments did we miss this week?" "Block off Friday morning."
03
Review before it acts
For anything that sends or changes data, the agent shows you a preview first. You approve, tweak the wording, or cancel — nothing goes out without your say.
04
Done — next task
The action completes and a log entry is saved to your session history. Keep going or close the tab — the agent picks up where you left off next time.

Billing Integration

An add-on, not a new plan

The Executive Agent sits on top of any existing subscription — it doesn't replace the Voice Agent or require a separate account. Owners toggle it on from the Billing page and it appears as a new line item on their Stripe invoice, the same way future AI employees will be billed.

How the add-on works: A single "Enable Executive Agent" toggle appears in the Billing section. Turning it on adds a fixed monthly charge to the next invoice — suggested $49–$79/mo. Usage (sessions and minutes) is shown in the Billing page alongside the existing call usage bar. Turning it off cancels the add-on at the end of the billing period with no penalty. All of this is handled through Stripe subscription items — the same infrastructure already powering the existing plans.
